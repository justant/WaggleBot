WaggleBot Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

1. Information We Collect

WaggleBot is a self-hosted tool. It accesses only the data you explicitly authorize through platform OAuth connections (e.g., TikTok, YouTube).

  • TikTok: Basic user profile information (display name) and video upload permissions.
  • YouTube: Channel information, video upload, and analytics data.

2. How We Use Information

Collected data is used solely for:

  • Uploading video content to your connected accounts.
  • Displaying basic account information in the dashboard.
  • Collecting video performance analytics for optimization.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your own server. OAuth tokens are saved in local configuration files and are never transmitted to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

WaggleBot does not share, sell, or transfer your personal data to any third party. All processing occurs on your local machine.

5. Data Retention

You can delete all stored data at any time by removing the local configuration files (e.g., config/tiktok_token.json, config/credentials.json).

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data since WaggleBot is self-hosted. You may revoke platform access at any time through each platform's settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
